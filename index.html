Frequently Asked Questions

Event design and event planning work hand in hand to deliver a successful event.Events by Knight offer tailor made events. This means we incorporate both event design and event planning to create that perfect event.

The time to plan events varies from a few weeks to years. If your event is small, you can probably put it together in a month or two. A good rule of thumb for any major event, from weddings to a company holiday party, is six months.

Cancellations made 7 days or more in advance of the event date, will receive a 100% refund. Cancellations made within 3 - 6 days will incur a 20% fee. Cancellations made within 48 hours to the event will incur a 30% fee.

When event budgeting, it’s paramount to identify which items represent expenses and which are sources of revenue that can cover costs. In addition, we distinguish between fixed and variable costs in order to effectively forecast a budget for your next event.
